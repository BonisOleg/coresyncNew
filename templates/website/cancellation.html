Cancellation Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. Session Cancellations

We understand that plans can change. To ensure availability for all guests, we ask that you follow our cancellation guidelines:

  • 48+ hours before session: Full refund to your original payment method.
  • 24–48 hours before session: 50% refund or full credit toward a future session.
  • Less than 24 hours: No refund. The full session fee will be charged.
  • No-show: The full session fee will be charged with no refund.

2. How to Cancel

You may cancel your booking through any of the following methods:

  • Through the CoreSync mobile app (Bookings section).
  • By contacting our concierge via the website chat.
  • By sending a message via WhatsApp.

Cancellations are effective once you receive a confirmation message or email.

3. Rescheduling

You may reschedule your session free of charge up to 24 hours before the scheduled time, subject to availability. Rescheduling requests made less than 24 hours before the session are treated as cancellations.

4. Membership Cancellations

  • Annual memberships may be cancelled within 14 days of purchase for a full refund, provided no member benefits have been redeemed.
  • After the 14-day window, membership fees are non-refundable for the remainder of the membership term.
  • Membership will remain active until the end of the paid term.

5. Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days to your original payment method via Stripe. Wallet credit refunds are applied immediately.

6. Exceptions

We may make exceptions to this policy in cases of documented emergencies or extraordinary circumstances. Please contact our team directly to discuss your situation.

7. Contact Us

For cancellation requests or questions about this policy, please contact us at support@coresync.com.
