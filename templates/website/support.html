Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?

Open the CoreSync app, go to Profile, and tap "Delete Account". This permanently removes all your data.

How do I manage my payment methods?

Go to the Wallet tab in the app. You can add, remove, and set default payment methods.

How do I cancel a booking?

Open the booking from the Bookings tab and tap "Cancel Booking".

Is my payment information secure?

Yes. All payment processing is handled by Stripe, a PCI Level 1 certified payment processor. We never store your full card details.
