================================================================================
SECTION 1: GENERAL PRINCIPLES
================================================================================

The AI concierge is the primary interface for booking sessions.
The experience must feel like a guided concierge conversation, not a traditional form.

Key rules:
- Ask one thing at a time
- Use buttons or structured inputs whenever possible
- Avoid requiring free typing unless necessary
- Avoid asking multiple questions in one message
- Keep the background CoreSync room visible behind the AI panel
- No page redirects — everything happens inside the AI interface

Target booking time: 45–60 seconds


================================================================================
SECTION 2: BOOKING FLOW — STEP BY STEP
================================================================================


--- Step 1 — Initial Greeting ---

AI Message:
"Welcome to CoreSync Private.
I'm your concierge. How can I assist you today?"

Buttons:
• Book a session
• Explore membership
• Just exploring

If the user selects "Book a session", continue to Step 2.


--- Step 2 — Select Date ---

AI Message:
"When would you like to visit?"

A calendar picker opens.
User selects a date.
After selecting the date, continue to Step 3.


--- Step 3 — Select Time ---

AI Message:
"What time works best for you?"

Display available time slots for the selected date.
Example:
• 6:30 PM
• 8:30 PM
• 10:30 PM

The system should only show available time slots.
Users should not type times manually.


--- Step 3.5 — Experience Tier Selection ---

After time selection:

AI message:
"Your session includes the Full CoreSync Experience, prepared with food and refreshments."

Buttons:
• Full Experience — $X
• Experience without food — $Y

Small text below:
"Members receive preferred pricing."

Design rules:
- Full experience appears first
- Slight visual emphasis (bolder or highlighted)
- The cheaper option appears slightly more neutral

If Full Experience is selected:

AI:
"Would you prefer dairy or meat for your visit?"

Buttons:
• Dairy
• Meat

Then continue booking.


--- Step 4 — Guest Name ---

AI Message:
"What name should I reserve the session under?"

Input fields:
- First Name
- Last Name

Button:
Continue


--- Step 5 — Phone Number & Verification ---

AI Message:
"What phone number should I send your confirmation to?"

Input field:
- Phone number

After submission:
System sends SMS verification code.

AI Message:
"Enter the 6-digit code I just sent."

Input field:
- Verification code

Purpose:
- confirm identity
- allow recognition for future bookings


--- Step 6 — Optional Email ---

AI Message:
"Would you like your confirmation and receipt sent to an email as well?"

Input field:
- Email address

Buttons:
• Add Email
• Skip

Email is optional.
If provided, it will be used for:
- confirmation email
- receipt
- calendar invitation


--- Step 7 — Booking Summary, Terms & Payment ---

AI Message:
"You're reserving CoreSync Private for:"

Display booking summary:
- CoreSync Private
- Date
- Time
- Duration
- Price

Below that:
☑ I agree to the Terms & Conditions and Cancellation Policy

Links:
- Terms & Conditions
- Cancellation Policy

Then the Pay button.

Payment options:
• Pay with Card
• Apple Pay / Google Pay (if available)

Payment must be processed through a secure payment provider (ex: Stripe).

Optional message after payment:
"Would you like me to remember this card for faster booking next time?"

If accepted:
- store payment token securely through the payment processor.


--- Step 8 — Booking Confirmation ---

AI Message:
"Your session is confirmed."

Display:
- Session Details
- Date
- Time
- Confirmation number

Buttons:
• Add to Calendar
• View Membership
• Done

If email was provided:
Send:
- confirmation email
- receipt
- calendar file (.ics)

SMS confirmation should also be sent to the verified phone number.


--- Step 8.5 — Environment Selection (Post-Confirmation) ---

After payment confirmation the AI says:
"Your session is confirmed.
Would you like to select the environment for your visit?"

Then show options like:
• Italy
• Japan
• Iceland
(These correspond to the screen environments / scenes.)

User taps one.

AI confirms:
"Perfect. Your environment is set to Italy.
You can change it anytime before your visit."

If they do nothing:
Default scene loads.


--- Step 8.6 — WhatsApp Contact ---

After booking confirmation, provide:
Link to WhatsApp for contact support if needed.

NOTE: No separate "contact section" exists. WhatsApp link is ONLY shown after booking.


================================================================================
SECTION 3: RETURNING GUEST FLOW
================================================================================

If the phone number already exists:

AI Message:
"Welcome back, [First Name].
Would you like to book the same experience as last time?"

Buttons:
• Same time again
• Choose new time

This enables faster repeat bookings.


================================================================================
SECTION 4: PRE-SESSION SMS REMINDER
================================================================================

1–2 hours before the session the system sends SMS:
"Your CoreSync session begins soon.
Would you like to set your environment?"

Link opens AI again (concierge interface).
This builds anticipation.


================================================================================
SECTION 5: MEMBERSHIP FLOW
================================================================================


--- 5.1 Membership Upsell Moments ---

The AI should behave like a luxury concierge, not a pushy salesperson.
Three ideal moments to present membership:

MOMENT 1 — During Exploration:
User asks questions like: "how does it work?" / "how often can I come?"
AI replies with subtle positioning:
"Many guests choose membership because it guarantees priority access and preferred booking windows."
Then offer:
• Learn about membership
• Book a session first

MOMENT 2 — After Booking:
This is the highest conversion moment.
After confirmation:
"Members receive priority booking and preferred access to CoreSync Private.
Would you like to explore membership?"
Buttons:
• View membership
• Maybe later
(People who already booked are much more likely to convert.)

MOMENT 3 — Returning Guests:
If the system recognizes a returning guest:
"Welcome back, [Name].
Guests who visit regularly often choose membership for priority access."
Buttons:
• Show membership
• Book session


--- 5.2 AI Sales Philosophy ---

The AI should NEVER say:
"Buy membership."

Instead say:
"Many guests choose membership…"
"Members receive…"
"Would you like to explore…"

This feels premium and non-pushy.


--- 5.3 Membership Teaser (Pre-Click) ---

On the page itself (before clicking membership), show this small teaser:
"CoreSync Private Club
Priority access and privileges for returning couples.
Founding memberships limited to 100."

This teaser alone will make people curious.


--- 5.4 Full Membership Flow ---

STEP 1 — When the user clicks "Membership":

AI:
"CoreSync Private Club is designed for couples who plan to return and want priority access to the CoreSync experience.
Would you like me to briefly explain how membership works?"

Buttons:
• Yes, show me
• Maybe later

STEP 2 — Membership Reveal:

If they click Yes:

AI:
"Membership gives couples preferred access and privileges at CoreSync."

Then display the key benefits:
Members receive:
• 1 Essential Ritual included each year
• Preferred member session rates
• Priority booking access for high-demand times
• Access to the CoreSync app to personalize the experience
• One complimentary house bottle during each visit
• Preferred pricing on premium bottles, cigars, and ritual gifts
• $250 birthday credit each year
• Guest booking privilege
• Early access to new CoreSync experiences and the backyard

Then the scarcity line:
"Founding Membership is limited to the first 100 couples."

STEP 3 — Price Reveal:

AI:
"Annual membership is $2,200."

Then immediately reinforce value:
"Many members recover the cost of membership within their first few visits while gaining priority access to the experience."

STEP 4 — Soft Invitation:

AI:
"Would you like me to reserve a CoreSync membership for you?"

Buttons:
• Yes, reserve membership
• I want to learn more
• Not right now

STEP 5 — If They Click "Learn More":

AI:
"Members typically join because they plan to visit multiple times and want priority booking access, preferred pricing, and personalized control of the experience through the CoreSync app."

Then:
"Founding memberships are limited to 100 couples."

Buttons:
• Reserve membership
• Continue browsing

STEP 6 — If They Click "Reserve Membership":

AI:
"Would you like to activate membership today or add it when booking your first experience?"

Buttons:
• Activate membership now
• Add it to my booking

SECTION 6: IMAGE GALLERY INTERACTION

Then show 3–4 curated photos.

AI immediately continues:
"Would you like to reserve a session?"

Buttons:
• Book a session
• Tell me more
• Explore membership

This keeps momentum.


================================================================================
SECTION 7: INTERFACE INTERACTION RULES
================================================================================

The AI interface must support three interaction types:

1. Buttons — for predictable choices
2. Structured inputs — for personal data (name, phone, email, verification code)
3. Free typing — only for open-ended questions

Each step should prioritize structured interaction over typing.


================================================================================
SECTION 8: PAYMENT SECURITY
================================================================================

Credit card details must never be stored by the website directly.

Use a payment processor (ex: Stripe) that provides:
- secure checkout
- tokenized card storage
- Apple Pay / Google Pay support


================================================================================
SECTION 9: IDENTITY & REBOOKING
================================================================================

No user accounts or passwords are required (for web).
Returning guests are recognized via:
- phone number
- SMS verification code

This allows fast rebooking without logins.


================================================================================
SECTION 10: FLUTTER APP FEATURES
================================================================================

The app needs to control the following:
1. Screen scenes and music add-on (if they want)
2. Scent control
3. Checking in
4. Order add-ons (whiskey, flowers, etc.) with a message like "happy birthday" etc.
5. Booking
6. Session timer
7. Payment wallet

Basics — these are already partially implemented in the codebase.

================================================================================

No forms. No redirects. Just a conversation with structured inputs.
Target: 45–60 seconds from start to payment confirmation.
